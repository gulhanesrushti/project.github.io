Title-Swami Charitra

Author- Shree Vishnu Balavat Thorat

Swami Maharaj transforms into pure bliss and happiness the material and spiritual life of a devotee for whom Shree Swami Samarth Maharaj alone is the very essence of life, its very cause and manifestion in every sense. Late Shree Vishnu Balwant Thorat composed and published this little holy scripture in AD 1897.

Shyamchi Aai

Author- Sane Guruji

The plot revolves around a boy called Shyam and his relationship with his mother. It shares the effect Shyam's mother has had on Shyam's life and upbringing, and how he is taught to stick to his ideals and principles, even in the face of poverty. The relationships of all the individuals in Shyam's family are explored.

Title-Bilieve in yourself

Author- Jpseph murphy

In Believe in Yourself, Dr. Murphy stresses about having faith in ones abilities, believing in the inner self and in having the courage to chase your dream, come what may. The book was first published in 1955 but remains as popular as it was then. Being a preacher, with decades of experience behind him, Dr Murphy delves into lives of people to demonstrate the all encompassing power of self.

Title-it starts with us

Author- Collen Hover

It Starts with Us is a romance novel by Colleen Hoover, published by Atria Books on October 18, 2022.[1] It is the sequel to her 2016 best-selling novel It Ends with Us.[2] The sequel was first announced in February 2022.[3] It became Simon & Schuster's most pre-ordered book of all time.[4] Hoover wrote the novel as a "thank you" to fans of the first novel.[5].
